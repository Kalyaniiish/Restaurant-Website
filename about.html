About Us

Welcome to Urban Tandoor, where every meal is a celebration of flavor, culture, and community.

Born out of a passion for great food and warm hospitality, we bring together the finest ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a modern twist to create dishes that delight the senses.

Our chefs craft each plate with care, using locally sourced produce and authentic spices to ensure every bite is fresh and full of flavor. From our signature dishes like Wood-fired Pizzas to Handcrafted Pastas, there’s something on our menu for everyone.

Urban Tandoor is more than just a place to eat — it’s a place to connect. Our team is here to serve with heart, and our space is designed to make you feel at home.

Come hungry. Leave happy.
